About the team

The dewpoint project team is composed of four School of the Art Institute of Chicago students whose studies focus in Art and Technology department. With a base in Art and Technology each member also contributed unique skills in designing the dewpoint project such as 3D modeling, 3D fabrication, digital rendering, graphic design, product design, audio and video, and web design.

Bailey Beatt is a fourth year undergraduate student who focuses in 3D design and fabrication, virtual reality, sound, and lighting design.

Maurice Hampton is a fourth year undergraduate student who focuses in robotics, computer vision, and the internet of things.

Jackie Huang is a third year undergraduate student who focuses in 3D design, wood and plastic fabrication, motion graphics, and lighting design.

Sam Scheib is a third year undergraduate student who focuses in lighting design, graphic design, and fashion design.
